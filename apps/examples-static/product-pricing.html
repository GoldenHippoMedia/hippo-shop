Product pricing — declarative bindings

This page is 100% declarative except a single 12-line script that registers a custom saveBlurb formatter. Every value below comes from one product fetch — the SDK dedupes identical reads across the page. Bindings update in place when window.gh.refresh() is called, which is how a newly registered formatter takes effect without a reload.

How this works

data-field="reviews.globalFiveStarReviews"
Cross-brand 5-star count — real DTO field that most demos overlook.
data-format="number:1"
Built-in numeric formatter; the argument is fraction-digit count.
data-format="saveBlurb:USD"
Custom formatter registered via window.gh.format.register.
data-attr-href="slug"
Per-attribute binding — sets href from the product slug.
data-attr-data-product-id="id"
Sets a data-* attribute on the CTA for analytics tooling.
data-if="variants.subscription.standardByQuantity.6.alternatePurchaseTypePrice"
Hides the "or one-time at $X" line cleanly when the alternate price is null.
data-field="…6.defaultFrequency.label"
Subscription cadence pulled straight off the variant's frequency object.
